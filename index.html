dongwoo.kim @ postech.ac.kr

Dongwoo Kim is an associate professor at POSTECH, leading the machine learning lab. His research primarily focuses on the geometric structure of datasets and models, and how they influence machine learning algorithms. He has a history of working as a lecturer at the Australian National University and holds a Ph.D. and M.S. degrees from KAIST, as well as a B.E. from Sungkyunkwan University.
