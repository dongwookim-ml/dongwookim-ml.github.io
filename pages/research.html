Research Themes

Our research explores the geometry of data and models across multiple areas.

Graph Neural Networks

Understanding and improving GNNs through geometric perspectives, addressing over-smoothing, and developing novel architectures.

  • Reverse Process GNN for Heterophilic Graphs – ICML 2024

  • Bridging Generalization and Expressivity – ICLR 2025

  • Local Vertex Colouring GNN – ICML 2023

Geometric Deep Learning

Leveraging hyperbolic and Riemannian geometry for better representation learning of hierarchical and structured data.

  • Hyperbolic VAE via Latent Gaussian – NeurIPS 2023

  • Rotated Hyperbolic Wrapped Normal – NeurIPS 2022

Trustworthy AI

Developing methods for machine unlearning, privacy-preserving ML, and robust evaluation of AI systems.

  • Holistic Unlearning Benchmark – ICCV 2025

  • Few-shot Unlearning – S&P 2024

  • Robust Evaluation of Diffusion Purification – ICCV 2023 (Oral)

ML for Science

Applying machine learning to scientific discovery, including drug design, molecular modeling, and computational biology.

  • High-order Equivariant Flow Matching – NeurIPS 2025 (Spotlight)

  • Multi-Reward Drug Design – ICML 2025

Language Models

Research on LLM personalization, retrieval-augmented generation, and geometric reasoning in vision-language models.

  • CoPL: Collaborative Preference Learning – EMNLP 2025

  • RAG with Source Reliability – EMNLP 2025

  • GeoDANO: Geometric VLM – EMNLP 2025

Generative Models

Advancing graph generation, diffusion models, and generative approaches for structured data.

  • Hierarchical Graph Generation with K2-trees – ICLR 2024

  • Feature Unlearning for GANs and VAEs – AAAI 2024
