How to Join?

For Prospective Graduate Students

  1. I can supervise students from CSE and GSAI. You may apply to either program.

  2. Gain research experience: POSTECH undergrads can join through CSED399/499. Currently no openings for external students.

  3. Apply to POSTECH Graduate School: CSE | GSAI

  4. After admission: Send your CV via email.

  5. Lab interview: Schedule will be announced.

Undergraduate Research

Interested undergraduates can participate in our research through POSTECH's CSED399/499 program. You will be paired with a PhD student mentor and work on ongoing projects.
